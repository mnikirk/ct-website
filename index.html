ConsulTech Technologies

ConsulTech is one of Bloomington's oldest Internet Service Providers (ISPs), in continuous operation since 1995. With offices near College Mall in Bloomington, we are a local company, dedicated to helping local companies and organizations focus their resources on the things that make them successful.

Our goal is to be the pre-eminent provider of Information Technology outsourcing services to small and medium-sized businesses and not-for-profit organizations in South Central Indiana, and to be viewed as valued partners by our clients.

Services

ISP Services

ConsulTech provides internet services to many high-profile apartment complexes throughout Bloomington across fiber optic, wireless and copper connections.


IT Consulting

ConsulTech provides high quality, cost-effective services to companies and organizations that wish to outsource some or all of their technology needs. We provide system administration, web site hosting, e-mail hosting and support, business PC and network support, wireless computing, Internet security services, apartment network management, and general IT consulting.

History

ConsulTech is a privately-held company based in Bloomington, Indiana. Founded in 1995 by two technologist/entrepreneurs from Indiana University, the company established a profitable niche by providing network connectivity and support to its clients, a significant number of whom were in the real estate industry. In continuous operation since 1995, ConsulTech is one of Bloomington's oldest internet service providers (ISPs).
